Welcome

DeveXe is a software engineering consultancy specialising in Java, XML, Web and Semantic Web technologies. We offer a comprehensive range of services including systems analysis, design, development, installation, support and administration.

We have over 15 years experience of developing information technology solutions for a broad range of organisations including multinational corporations, SMEs, National Government, local authorities, sole traders and charities.

DeveXe is strongly committed to crafting clean, robust and maintainable code while remaining firmly focussed on your business needs and delivering on target.

Wherever appropriate we make use of international standards such as those developed by the W3C, WAI, OASIS and the OGC. We are also committed to using open source software wherever possible.
